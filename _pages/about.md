👋 Hello and Welcome. This is Youngjin's Personal Homepage 👋 
======
![Illustration of combining vision and language modalities](/images/meReal.jpg){: .align-right width="300px"}
I am a postdoctoral researcher in Computer Science (Supervisor: [Dr. Karthik Dantu](https://www.buffalo.edu/cubs/members.host.html/content/shared/engineering/computer-science-engineering/profiles/faculty/ladder/dantu-karthik.html))  with a Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering (Supervisor: Dr. [Tarunraj Singh](https://engineering.buffalo.edu/mechanical-aerospace/people/faculty/t-singh.html)). 

My work bridges the fields of control theory, robotics, and autonomous systems. I'm passionate about exploring advanced control methods like optimal control, nonlinear control, and safety-critical control to tackle challenging problems in autonomous systems.

I have hands-on experience with commercial robotic platforms and love designing custom testing devices to bring innovative research ideas to life. My current research focuses on developing model-based control strategies to improve the performance, efficiency, and safety of autonomous systems.
